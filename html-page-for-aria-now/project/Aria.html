Everything your team needs, live

From the first ring to the final transcript, Aria gives you full visibility and control over every conversation.

Real-time Transcription

Every word captured as it's spoken. Searchable transcripts available seconds after each call ends, with speaker labels and timestamps.

Intelligent Routing

AI matches every caller to the best available agent based on topic, sentiment, history, and skill — before anyone says hello.

Sentiment Analysis

Spot frustrated callers in real time. Aria flags negative sentiment mid-call so supervisors can step in before things escalate.

Auto Summaries

AI-generated call summaries pushed to your CRM the moment a call ends. No manual notes, no missed follow-ups.

Quality Scoring

Every call scored automatically against your rubric. Track compliance, empathy, and resolution rate across your whole team.

Live Analytics

Dashboards that refresh every second. Monitor queue depth, handle time, and CSAT without waiting for the end-of-day report.
